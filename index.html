Our Story

Potion Gazeuse began with five high school girls who shared a love for bold flavors and big dreams. What started as experimenting with dirty soda combinations after school quickly became a real business idea.

We wanted to create something playful yet elevated — drinks that feel aesthetic, fun, and unforgettable. Each flavor is crafted with creativity, friendship, and ambition.
